# CRACKL Terms Of Service Draft

This is a launch draft for legal review before public release.

## Service

CRACKL is a competitive riddle and puzzle arena with solo modes, multiplayer rooms, challenge links, progression, virtual coins/Intels, and admin-curated content.

## Accounts

Users are responsible for maintaining account security and for activity under their account. CRACKL may suspend accounts involved in cheating, abuse, automated play, exploit attempts, or harassment.

## Gameplay And Virtual Items

Coins/Intels, XP, ranks, wagers, streaks, and rewards are virtual gameplay systems unless a separate legally reviewed payout system is explicitly enabled. CRACKL may rebalance rewards, modes, or progression to preserve fairness.

## User Conduct

Users may not:

- Reverse engineer, scrape, automate, or attack the service.
- Abuse challenge links, wagers, rooms, or bounty attempts.
- Upload malicious content or attempt to access admin systems.
- Harass, impersonate, or threaten other users.

## Admin Content

Riddles, media, designs, and gameplay content are owned or licensed by CRACKL or its operators. Admins must ensure uploaded content does not violate third-party rights.

## Availability

CRACKL may go offline for maintenance, updates, security response, or infrastructure issues. No uptime guarantee is provided unless a separate agreement says otherwise.
